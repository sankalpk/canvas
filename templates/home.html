Why Speech

Public speaking is the number one fear in America. Conquering this fear will allow you to master your interviews, give perfect presentations, and deliver captivating speeches. CMU Speech and Debate is your gateway to turn America's greatest fear into your greatest asset.

Fun Workshops

Our interactive workshops are both fun and valuable learning experiences. Our workshops provide you with access to personalized coaching, feedback from our team, and multiple chances to practice speaking at every meeting.

Exciting Competitions

Speech and Debate competitions are intense and exciting. Competitors have to be skilled orators that can deliver beautiful rhetoric while also thinking quickly on their feet. Our workshops and coaching sessions will prepare CMU students to excel at both.

Personalized Coaching

This is for those of us that don’t want to compete and don’t have time for weekly workshops. Our personalized coaching sessions provide CMU students with an opportunity to receive feedback on their class presentations, case competitions, and more.
